What We Do

Data Intelligence

We develop technology and processes that oil and gas industry organizations can apply to capture and transform raw data into insights that can improve value, yield, safety and environmental protection. Our products capture the information needed to predict business outcomes and to make real time decisions that are critical in outperforming competition.

Our objective is to successfully harness the huge potential of data analytics, in order to help oil and gas organizations achieve optimal operational efficiency.

Software Solutions

We create user friendly, intuitive oil and gas software that enables organizations to use the latest advances in data analysis techniques, and provides them with the tools needed to answer critical questions impacting daily productivity and overall success.

Machine Learning

Machine learning allows us to create systems that can analyze and learn from data, rather than follow only explicitly programmed instructions. Such systems use the patterns and trends found in data to detect and predict situations that cannot be otherwise described by simple rules.

Data Analysis

We use quality-tested algorithms, constantly updated to reflect the latest statistical methodologies. This enables us to analyze the past and describe the current situation - all based on readily available data.
